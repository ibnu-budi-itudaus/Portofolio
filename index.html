PKG (Penilaian Kinerja Guru) APP

PKG APP is a decision support system that can assist schools in carrying out Teacher Performance Assessments, and is able to sort teachers based on their performance assessment from highest to lowest automatically. The criteria assessed in this teacher performance assessment are based on competencies that have been determined by the government, including pedagogical competency, personality competency, social competency and professional competency, of which there are 14 sub-competencies or criteria. The method used in this research is Weighted Product ( WP). Weighted Product (WP) is used to determine the highest alternative value or the highest teacher performance value based on the weights that have been determined by the ranking process. The results of the Weighted Product (WP) method calculation process from this software can determine teachers with the best performance based on teacher performance assessments quickly and accurately, so that the decision-making process is more targeted, effective and efficient.
